About

I am an embedded software engineer and technical project manager with over 10 years of industry experience at LG Electronics, where I worked on large-scale Smart TV platforms, global OTT integrations, and data-driven service operations.

My career began in biological research, where I developed Java-based 3D reconstruction tools for cellular imaging using TEM and HVEM. This early exposure to computational modeling later became the foundation for my transition into software engineering and embedded systems.

At LG Electronics, I contributed as a software engineer on Smart TV recording systems and later served as a technical project manager, leading cross-functional initiatives across engineering, data analytics, privacy compliance (GDPR/CCPA), and global service operations spanning more than 50 countries.

I am currently expanding my formal computer science training through graduate-level coursework, including Harvard CS50x, Stanford’s Machine Learning with Graphs (XCS224W) , and UC San Diego Extension courses in Java and Statistics.

My technical interests sit at the intersection of software systems, data, and applied machine learning, with a practical focus on building scalable, privacy-aware platforms rather than academic research.
